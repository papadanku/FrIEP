
# Adaptive Assessments

## Vineland Adaptive Behavior Scales 路 Third Edition 路 Multi-Rater

The Vineland is a comprehensive norm-referenced measure for assessing the adaptive behavior of individuals from birth through adulthood. Adaptive behavior refers to the individual's typical performance of daily activities. These scales, therefore, assess an individual's actual performance as opposed to his or her potential.

- High Score Ranges: 21-24 (Scale Scores), 130-140 (Standard Scores)
- Moderately High Score Ranges: 18-20 (Scale Scores), 115-129 (Standard Scores)
- Adequate Score Ranges: 13-17 (Scale Scores), 86-114 (Standard Scores)
